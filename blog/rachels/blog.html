Introduction

 

In the recently recorded episode of the "Can You Feel So Now" podcast, host Justin Barton sat down with Rachel Stumm, a recently-returned missionary of the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints who shared her inspiring journey of faith, courage, and personal growth. Rachel's story is a testament to the power of trusting in Christ and using our agency to navigate life's uncertainties.

 

A Life Dedicated to Service

 

From Spokane, Washington, Rachel's journey began with her decision to embark on a mission to serve the Lord which evolved in ways she hadn't expected. Initially called to serve in the Michigan, Lansing Mission, circumstances led her to complete her service as a service missionary in Spokane, Washington. Rachel candidly shared her journey through unexpected changes, the struggles she faced, and her faith in God's plan.

 

 

Embracing Change and Challenges

 

Rachel's journey was not without its challenges. She spoke about her struggles with mental health and the lessons she learned through her mission experience. Her story highlights the courage it takes to let go of personal desires and allow God to guide her path. Rachel found peace and joy in accepting the call to serve in a way that was unconventional but deeply fulfilling.

 

The Role of Courage

 

Throughout the podcast, Rachel emphasized the importance of courage in her mission. For her, courage meant allowing God to use her gifts for His work. Despite having dealt with mental health issues, her mission taught her that courage involves a willingness to step out of comfort zones and trust in the Lord's plan.

 

 

Learning to Trust and Find Certainty in Christ

 

Rachel's experience taught her the significance of acting with faith even when certainty seemed elusive. Through moments of loneliness and self-doubt, she found herself learning to trust in Jesus Christ as the constant in her life. Her story illustrates how repeated acts of trust can lead to profound certainty and assurance in one's faith journey.

 

The Power of Consecration and Agency

 

One of the pivotal lessons Rachel shared was the transformative power of agency and consecration. She shed light on how missionary work, for her, became a reflection of her authentic self, using her natural talents and gifts to bless others. Rachel's insights remind us that by consecrating our time and efforts, we allow God to work miracles through us, even when the path is unclear.

 

Lasting Joy Through Faith

 

Rachel eloquently expressed how the joy she now experiences transcends circumstances. Her understanding of true joy, aligned with her faith in God, allows her to navigate life's ups and downs. With humility and gratitude, she has embraced the role of Christ in her life, fostering a deeper, more lasting joy.

 

A Testament of Faith

 

In her heartfelt testimony at the end of the episode, Rachel affirmed her unwavering belief in Jesus Christ, the Book of Mormon, and the power of faith. Her words resonated with the essence of the podcast's theme—finding reassurance and strength in our Savior during life's most challenging moments.

 

 

Conclusion

 

Rachel Stumm's journey serves as an inspiring reminder of the courage and faith required to embrace an unexpected path. Her story encourages us to lean on our faith, trust in God's plan, and embrace the beauty of our individual journeys. As listeners reflect on Rachel's experiences, they are reminded of the enduring joy that comes from choosing Christ and trusting Him with their whole heart.
