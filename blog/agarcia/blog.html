Introduction

 

In this insightful podcast episode of Can You Feel So Now? Podcast, host Justin Barton sat down with Alaya Garcia to delve into her transformative journey of serving a mission for the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints. Through candid conversation, Alaya shared her experiences, struggles, and revelations, offering a heartfelt glimpse into her spiritual growth.

 

 

Alaya's Introduction

 

Alaya Garcia, a recently returned missionary who served in the Washington, Spokane Mission, introduced herself to the audience. Raised in Harlingen, Texas, Alaya comes from a family of five, with three younger sisters, and a love for crafts and sports. Her mission experience began in September 2023 and concluded in March 2025, marking a pivotal chapter in her life.

 

 

The Decision to Serve

 

Reflecting on her decision to serve, Alaya shared how her childhood conversations about missions and her unique experiences as one of the few youth members in her high school fueled her desire to serve. It was these discussions and interactions that bolstered her determination to share her faith and live by gospel standards.

 

 

Facing Challenges and Finding Strength

 

Throughout her mission, Alaya encountered numerous challenges, both mental and emotional. From adjusting to life away from family to confronting the demands of missionary work, she found solace and guidance in the resource "Adjusting to Missionary Life." Journaling became a key coping mechanism, helping her process her emotions and find gratitude in daily experiences.

 

 

Life Lessons from Mission Presidents

 

Serving under two mission presidents, Alaya gained valuable insights. The first emphasized obedience and baptism, urging missionaries to "repent at the speed of thought." This instilled in Alaya the importance of striving for exactness in her faith. The second president focused on the doctrine of Christ and lifelong discipleship, teaching Alaya the importance of continuously applying gospel principles in daily life.

 

 

Understanding Jesus Christ and Personal Testimony

 

Alaya's mission profoundly deepened her understanding of Jesus Christ. Initially perceiving Him as a historical figure, she grew to see Christ as a personal savior and brother. This realization empowered her to rely on His atonement for strength during difficulties, transforming her testimony into a vibrant, living force in her life.

 

 

Conclusion: Embracing the Song of Redeeming Love

 

As Alaya concluded the podcast with her heartfelt testimony, she emphasized the enduring power of faith and gratitude. By asking reflective questions and recognizing God's hand in daily life, Alaya maintains her connection to divine guidance. Her journey illustrates the profound impact of missions, not only in serving others but also in nurturing a deeper relationship with Jesus Christ.

 

Alaya's story reminds us of the transformative power of faith, service, and self-reflection. Her insights encourage us to seek God’s presence in our lives and to share the joy of redemption and love with those around us.
